My Erasmus Experience

Erasmus experience gave me an opportunity to improve my academic, personal, and communication skills in an international environment.

New Environment

During Erasmus I had a chance to study in a different country, meet people from various cultures, and adapt to a new academic system. This experience helped me become more independent and confident.

Skills I Improved

I improved my English communication, teamwork, problem-solving, and adaptability. Erasmus also helped me better understand how to work in international and multicultural teams.

Personal Growth

This experience was important not only for studies but also for my personal growth. It encouraged me to be more open-minded, responsible, and motivated to continue learning and exploring new opportunities.
